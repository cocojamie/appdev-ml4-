Hair Day

Based in Madison, Wisconsin, Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color and styling. Hair Day also offers makeup and nail services, prides itself on its warm and relaxing atmosphere and is best known for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts.

Our Services

Men's Haircut

A men's haircut typically involves trimming or cutting the hair on the head to maintain a neat and stylish appearance. There are various types of haircuts for men, such as the classic taper, fade, undercut, crew cut, and pompadour. The choice of haircut depends on personal preference, facial features, and hair type. To get the desired haircut, it's essential to communicate your preferences with your hairstylist and maintain regular appointments for upkeep.

Nail's Cut

A nail cutter, also known as nail clippers, is a tool used to trim fingernails and toenails. It usually has a hinged design with two handles and a small blade in between. Remember to use clean nail clippers and trim your nails regularly to maintain proper hygiene and avoid any potential issues.

Body Massage

Body massage is a therapeutic practice that involves manipulating the muscles and soft tissues of the body to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and alleviate pain. It can be performed using various techniques such as Swedish massage, deep tissue massage, or sports massage, among others. These techniques aim to improve blood circulation, increase flexibility, and enhance overall well-being. Regular body massages can provide numerous benefits, including better sleep, improved mood, and increased range of motion.
